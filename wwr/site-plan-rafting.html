Overview

Purpose

Rafting and whitewater rafting are recreational outdoor activities which use an inflatable raft to navigate a river or other body of water. This is often done on whitewater or different degrees of rough water. Dealing with risk is often a part of the experience.

Audience

Who are the target participants ? The Rafting activities include people for different category like youngs and adults who are in excellent physical condition and who have completed our prep program of training in order to familiarize themselves with essential equipment such as paddles and personal flotation devices. What our participants want? Our participants want to enjoy their rafting adventure offer varying levels of intensity What do our participants expect to find? We need to provide them a best rafting experience in a safety conditions. There is always some degree of risk involved white water rafting due to the naturally volatile nature of the territory. All necessary safety precautions will be taken to provide an enjoyable trip.
